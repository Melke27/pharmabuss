# Short Financial Plan (Simple Version)

## Business Model (General)
- Free plan to attract users
- Paid plans: $1, $2, $3 per month
- Extra income from clinics/pharmacies: $50-$300 per year each

## Offline + Online Plan (Countryside Included)
- App works in both online and low-internet areas
- Offline mode: save reminders locally on phone
- SMS reminders for users without internet
- Voucher/scratch card payments for users without bank/mobile app
- Sync data automatically when internet comes back
- Clinic/pharmacy agents can register and help users in rural areas

## Payment Methods (Ethiopia Friendly)
- Telebirr
- CBE Birr
- Bank transfer
- Voucher/scratch card

## Needed Payment APIs (Only Essentials)
1. Create payment
2. Verify payment
3. Webhook/callback (payment status)
4. Renewal flow (monthly)
5. Transaction report

## Small Budget Plan (Corrected)
- One-time setup: **$30-$60** (use free/open-source tools)
- Monthly running cost:
  - Hosting/basic maintenance: **$20-$40**
  - SMS + support + outreach: **$20-$40**
- Total monthly cost: **$40-$80**

## Final Money Calculation (Example)
- Paid users: 300 users on $1 plan = **$300/month**
- B2B income: 6 partners x $100/year = $600/year = **$50/month**
- Total monthly income = **$350**
- Monthly cost (small budget) = **$60** (midpoint)
- **Estimated monthly profit = $290**

## Total Corrected Payment Needed (App to Work)

- One-time setup cost: **$30-$60**
- Monthly running cost: **$40-$80**
- **Total first month needed (setup + monthly): $70-$140**
- Recommended practical first month budget (midpoint): **$105**

### Year-1 Total Cost
- Minimum: $30 + (12 x $40) = **$510**
- Midpoint: $45 + (12 x $60) = **$765**
- Maximum: $60 + (12 x $80) = **$1,020**

This means you should prepare about **$105** to start (first month), and around **$765** for a full year on a small realistic budget.

## Free Options to Reduce Cost
- Use free/open-source stack for app and backend
- Start with free cloud tier
- Use WhatsApp/Telegram/community groups for free marketing
- Use digital vouchers first before printing physical cards

## Short Conclusion
Start free + low-price paid model, support offline countryside users, keep monthly cost under $80, and target early profit with local payments.
